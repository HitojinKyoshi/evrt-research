# EVRT Paper Index

## Emergent Vacuum Response Theory Research Program

This directory contains the organized EVRT (Emergent Vacuum Response Theory) paper series and associated Coherent Nonequilibrium Electromagnetic Systems research framework.

The EVRT series is a constrained phenomenological research program investigating whether structured nonequilibrium electromagnetic systems may exhibit measurable residual stress-energy effects under precision experimental conditions.

The program emphasizes:

- falsifiability
- conservation-law consistency
- artifact discrimination
- uncertainty-aware metrology
- reproducibility
- simulation-guided parameter analysis
- residual-force scaling
- null-result interpretation

This repository does **not claim discovery of new physics**, verified antigravity, or experimentally confirmed reactionless propulsion phenomena.

Instead, the series develops structured methodologies for evaluating whether candidate residual signatures remain after conventional physical explanations are rigorously constrained.
