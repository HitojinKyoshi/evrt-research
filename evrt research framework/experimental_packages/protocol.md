# Experimental Protocol (Baseline)

## Objective
Test for residual forces in asymmetric electromagnetic configurations.

## Setup
- High-voltage asymmetric capacitor or resonant cavity
- Vacuum chamber preferred (to eliminate ion wind)
- Force measurement via microbalance or torsion setup

## Controls
- Thermal drift monitoring
- Vibration isolation
- Electromagnetic shielding

## Measurement Strategy
- Record force vs time
- Compare powered vs unpowered states
- Perform repeated trials

## Expected Outcomes
- Null: no statistically significant force beyond noise
- Non-null: persistent signal requiring artifact elimination
