# Scientific Position

These roadmap figures are intended to illustrate the organizational structure and conceptual evolution of the EVRT research framework.

They do not imply experimental validation of anomalous-force effects or confirmed new physics.

The EVRT program remains a constrained phenomenological and experimental-metrology framework emphasizing falsifiability, uncertainty analysis, and artifact discrimination.
